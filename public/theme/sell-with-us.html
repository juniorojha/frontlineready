SELLING IS SIMPLE
  • Look at our selling options to decide if you would like to use our standard or concierge (currently UK only) service. to decide if you would like to use our standard or concierge service
  • Submit your entry online or use the form below to request a callback from a member of our team
  • We work with you to supply a photographer and you can include as many photos, videos and documents as you can (no limit)
  • Our team of automotive journalists research the vehicle and construct a professional listing. We approve the listing with you and agree the reserve
  • Auction goes live and we market extensively via magazines, digital media as well as our own extensive database of buyers
  • We eliminate the timewasters for you (buyers only come to collect once a deposit has been paid)

For those who want an even simpler way of selling, choose our 'Concierge' option (currently UK only) and we will provide professional photography, storage for your vehicle during the auction and deal with all viewings. If required we can even arrange transport on your behalf. Contact us for details.
