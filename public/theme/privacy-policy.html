PRIVACY POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Curating Cars launch pages and thank you for choosing to use our “keep up to date service” at this time. We have made our Privacy Policy easy and simple to understand at this stage so that you are informed as to how we will use your information prior to our full website going live. This Privacy Policy is important to us in order to maintain our integrity and compliance with regards to confidentiality in order to guarantee any data is fully protected as part of using our “keep up to date service”.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTED
PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION SUBMITTED BY YOU

You may not be required to provide us any information when you visit our launch pages. However, in order to receive our updates, exciting articles, what we have coming soon you will be required to submit basic personally identifiable information to us. Personally identifiable information is personal information that may be used to identify you as an individual so we can keep you informed in a professional manner. When using our launch pages, we may collect only your name, email and country of residence so we can consider how we communicate as our community grows.

Additionally, through our launch pages you can link to our social media allowing you to login through a third party social network, if you do so, you agree that we may collect any personally identifiable information you make available through following us.

NON-IDENTIFYING INFORMATION

Whenever you use our Site, App, or Service, we may collect non-identifying information from you, such as your IP address, interactions with the Site and Service, mobile carrier, query information, location, referring URL, browser, operating system, browser version, mobile device version, web address, geographic IP information, cookie information, usage, data transferred, and Internet Service Provider. Additionally, we may collect information regarding your usage and interaction with our launch pages. Without a legal submission request, voluntary compliance on the part of your Internet Service Provider, or additional records from a third party such as your wireless provider, this information alone cannot usually be used to identify you.

3. USE OF YOUR INFORMATION

We will never sell or transfer any personally identifiable information to third parties. However, you agree that we may use your information:

To enhance or improve our users’ experiences.

To provide our information service to you at this time.

To email you newsletters and correspondence.

To contact you and to respond to inquiries.

Additionally, we may give your information to law enforcement if we are compelled to by a court order, if there has been a violation of any laws or if a violation of the Privacy Policy has occurred.

4. ACCESSING, EDITING, AND REMOVING YOUR INFORMATION

If you have any questions or wish to review, change, or access any of your information collected by us, please contact us at contact@curatingcars.com. Additionally, if you wish to opt out of our data collection practices or would like us to remove any of your information, please contact us at contact@curatingcars.com. Please be aware that removal of your information means you will not get any updates or our cool articles on all things interesting in the world of cars and investments or what we have coming soon.

5. COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use cookies to remember you visited our launch pages so we can track the interest we receive during our early days, for this reason, you must have cookies enabled in your browser to use our launch pages and information services. Additionally, some or all of our cookies may be accessible by third parties. You can manage cookies yourself and learn more about them, including how to block them or delete them, at: http://www.direct.gov.uk/managingcookies. Additionally, more information about cookies may be found at http://www.aboutcookies.org/. Deleting cookies may cause functionality issues with our launch pages.
