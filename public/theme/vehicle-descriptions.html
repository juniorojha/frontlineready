Introduction

Described by Top Gear as “The most tactile Audi driver’s car ever” the Audi R8 was right at the top of the Audi range. It is a proper sports car, mixing V8 power with incredible handling, 4-wheel drive and beautiful looks. Out of the factory the R8 promised 420BHP from the 4.2 litre engine giving 0-60 in 4.4 seconds, and that’s in standard form!

This R8 has some serious upgrades including a supercharger, remote controlled Capristo exhaust, racing suspension and more. It's now said to be pushing out ~593BHP, that's some immense performance for this car. Modified to deliver on road and track this really is a fabulous performance car.

Exterior

Wheels & Tyres

Shaun advises the alloy wheels were recently refurbished and, as the images confirm, they remain in excellent condition. The Pirelli ZR19 ultra high performance tyres appear in great shape with good tread remaining, 295's on the rears to help transfer the power to the road.

Bodywork

Shaun took what was already a dramatic design and has added his own touches in the form of a rear wing and front splitter this year. The car stands square and straight. The panel gaps are good and the doors, boot and bonnet close cleanly and precisely, very typical of Audi engineering.

Paint

The car is wrapped in white and this has been done remarkably well. In fact, it is difficult to tell immediately that it has been wrapped. The original paint colour is black and where this is exposed, such as in the door shuts, the paint appears good. The new wrap was applied in September this year.

Glass & Trim

The glass appears in very good condition and the dark tint contrasts beautifully with the striking white wrap. The trim and badging is in excellent condition all round.

INterior

Seats & Carpets

The retrofit Recaro seats are in excellent condition and really enhance the sporty feel. These should provide good support for some spirited driving. The carpets, door cards and headlining all present very well with only some minor marks on the drivers door. The seats are new items, see the recent invoice at QST to see all the work completed.

Dashboard

The dashboard is clear and elegant with everything sensibly positioned. The glass is clear, without visible scratches and the dials are undamaged.

Steering Wheel / Gear Stick

The Steering wheel is in excellent shape, feeling secure and comfortable. The open gated gear shift and lever are beautifully designed, you are immediately drawn to them. The gear knob has typical signs of wear that come from it being a metal item.

Mechanics

Engine & Gearbox

The original specification 4.2 Litre V8 on this R8 is a robust, torquey and responsive unit. A sound basis then for adding the PES G4 FSI supercharger system which, combined with the exhaust, elevates the power output from the original 420BHP to just shy of ~600BHP.

As well as incredible performance the car sounds fantastic too with the remote control Capristo exhaust allowing you to enjoy the full, glorious, sound on the open road while toning it down nearer home to stay friends with your neighbours!

The engine starts well and idles steadily and evenly - check out the Mechanical Review to see the start up video.

Suspension & Brakes

The red brake calipers, visible through the dark alloy wheels look amazing and Shaun assures us they function pretty well too. The brake pads were replaced in 2014, and Shaun advises the calipers were recently overhauled.

The suspension on this car has been overhauled on this car with a Bilstein kit at a cost of ~£4,000 as detailed in the invoices.

The Drive

Shaun assures us the car drives as well as it looks and sounds. Lowered, with adjustable race suspension, the car is equipped to handle all the additional power. Testament to the cars engineering pedigree, Shaun describes the driving experience as intimate and engaging rather one of brutal raw power.

Electrics & Other

Shaun advises that all the electrics work as they should to his knowledge, with switches, lights and driving systems all fully functional. The car has Audi's own Navigation system built in.

History &
Paperwork

Past bills detail costs of over £12,000 on the supercharger and over £4,000 on the Capristo exhaust system. There are also various other bills showing considerable other investment in modifying this car. A rolling road report shows the power and torque readings of ~593BHP following the supercharger and exhaust installation.

Coming out of 9 years of ownership, with the upgrades completed in the current ownership, this is a car that is for those of us that look for that little bit extra.

We’ve done all our usual seller checks including photo ID, V5, and the vehicle is HPI clear.
